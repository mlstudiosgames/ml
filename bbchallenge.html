Ball Bounce Challenge!

Ball Bounce Challenge! is a ball bounce game where you must help to bounce the ball so it doesn't hit anything in these designer homes.

Bounce the ball in the house without hitting anything! Press the space bar to begin playing.

Version 2.1.3

Exactly two years ago from this date, on June 23rd, 2018, we released Ball Bounce Challenge to the public for the very first time.
